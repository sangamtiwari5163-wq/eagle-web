Our Story

A focused logistics startup for India and the UAE

Eagle Logistics & Relocations was founded to solve one specific problem: moving households and cargo between India and the UAE shouldn't mean juggling separate packers, freight agents and customs brokers who don't talk to each other.

Why We Started

One coordinator, instead of three different vendors

Families and businesses moving between India and the UAE typically deal with a local packer, a separate freight forwarder, and a customs agent for paperwork — with little coordination between them. Eagle Logistics & Relocations was set up to bring these pieces under one team, based in New Delhi.

We work directly with clients on domestic shifting within India as well, applying the same approach: clear communication, careful packing and a single point of contact from enquiry to delivery.

What Drives Us

Our Approach

As a newer company, we know trust is earned shipment by shipment. Every move and booking we handle is built around three commitments:

  • Clear communication — you know your shipment's status; we don't disappear after booking.
  • Honest pricing — itemised quotes, with duties and documentation costs explained upfront.
  • Careful handling — your belongings and cargo packed properly the first time.
