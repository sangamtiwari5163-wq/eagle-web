Our Services

Relocation and freight, with India ↔ UAE as our focus

From a single household move within India to a full international relocation or cargo shipment to the UAE, here's how we can help.

Within India

Domestic Relocation (India)

City-to-city household and office relocation across India — from packing at your current home to unloading and basic setup at the new one.

  • Professional packing materials for furniture, electronics and fragile items
  • Loading, transport and unloading coordinated end to end
  • Available for both household and office/commercial moves
Our Core Focus

India ↔ UAE Logistics

Moving between India and the UAE involves more moving parts than a domestic shift — freight booking, customs paperwork and coordination on both ends. This is the corridor we've built our service around.

  • Sea and air freight options between Indian ports/airports and the UAE
  • Guidance on import/export documentation required on both sides
  • Coordination for household relocation as well as commercial cargo
Moving Abroad

International Relocation

Beyond the UAE corridor, we coordinate international household relocation more broadly — packing at origin, freight booking, and handover at the destination address.

  • Pre-move planning and inventory listing for your shipment
  • Freight booking by sea or air depending on timeline and budget
  • Support coordinating delivery at the destination end
Packing & Handling

Household Shifting

Whether it's a one-room apartment or a full house, our team packs and handles your belongings with the same care across both domestic and international moves.

  • Room-by-room packing with labelled boxes for easy unpacking
  • Extra protection for fragile, valuable or sentimental items
  • Furniture disassembly and reassembly on request
Sea & Air

Freight Forwarding & Cargo Solutions

For commercial shipments, we help book and coordinate sea and air freight, sized to what you're moving — from a few boxes to larger commercial cargo.

  • Sea freight for larger, less time-sensitive shipments
  • Air freight for faster, time-sensitive cargo
  • Coordination with carriers and tracking of shipment milestones
Paperwork, Handled

Customs Clearance & Documentation Support

Customs paperwork is one of the most common places a shipment gets delayed. We help prepare and guide you through the documentation required for import and export.

  • Assistance with customs clearance paperwork at origin and destination
  • Guidance on import & export documentation requirements
  • Support compiling the paperwork carriers and authorities ask for
Across Every Service

Door-to-Door Logistics Solutions

Across all the services above, our goal is the same: you deal with one coordinator from pickup at origin to delivery at the final address — instead of separately managing a packer, a freight agent and a customs broker yourself.
