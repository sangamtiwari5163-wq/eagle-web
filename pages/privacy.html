Privacy Policy

How Eagle Logistics & Relocations collects, uses and protects your information.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Eagle Logistics & Relocations ("Eagle Logistics," "we," "us" or "our") collects, uses, discloses and safeguards information when you visit our website, request a quote, or use our relocation, freight forwarding or customs documentation support services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number and mailing address submitted through our contact or quote forms.
  • Shipment details: origin, destination, cargo description, volume and timing provided to generate quotes or coordinate services.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, device information and pages visited, collected automatically through standard web technologies.
  • Communications: records of correspondence between you and our team, including support and service-related enquiries.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Prepare and deliver relocation, freight forwarding and customs documentation quotes and services;
  • Communicate with you about enquiries, bookings, shipments and account matters;
  • Comply with customs, trade and other applicable legal or regulatory requirements;
  • Improve our website, services and customer experience;
  • Send service updates and, where you have opted in, occasional newsletters.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with carriers, customs authorities and relocation partners strictly as needed to fulfil a shipment or move, and with service providers who support our operations under confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and understand site usage. You can control cookie settings through your browser; disabling cookies may affect certain site features.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal and shipment information for as long as necessary to provide our services, meet legal, customs and tax record-keeping obligations, and resolve any disputes, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. International Data Transfers

Because we coordinate cross-border freight and relocation, information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including countries with different data protection laws. We take steps to ensure such transfers are conducted appropriately.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
